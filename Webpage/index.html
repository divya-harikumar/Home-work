Summary: Latitude vs X

The purpose of this project was to analyze the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator to showcase the following relationships:

Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude

We used Matplotlib to assess the various aspects of weather to measure the distance from the equator. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of analysis. It will also explain any trends and correlations witnessed.
