Latitude Vs Temperature Plot

The weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator. 

Latitude vs Humidity Plot

The southern hemisphere tends to be warmer than the northern hemisphere during this time of the year. This may be due to the tilt of earth.

Latitude Vs cloudiness Plot

There is no strong relationship between latitude and cloudiness. However, it is interesting to see that a strong band of cities sits at 0, 80, and 100% cloudiness.

Latitude Vs windspeed plot

There is no strong relationship between latitude and wind speed. However, in northern hemispheres there is a flurry of cities with over 20 mph of wind.
